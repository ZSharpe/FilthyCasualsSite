John Eichelberger

Since the age of 4, John’s been playing video games. The golden-age of his career was 1998 when Pokémon Red and Blue and the Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time were released in North America. John enjoys the RPG epic as well as complex story-driven games, with beloved-ties to the original Mass Effect trilogy, The Witcher franchise, and Halos 1-3. John is a competitive snob who takes things too seriously, beating all of your friends in Smash Bros. Ultimate and getting frustrated on Bloodborne speedruns.

When not playing video games, John enjoys reading classic literature, playing his guitar, and working on his Olympic lifts. He guitars and runs audio for a living, and loves making content that people will find joy in. He and his wife, Rebecca, live in a cozy one-bedroom apartment in Nashville, TN and are working hard to save money to purchase a home.

Top 3 Games

  • Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time
  • The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt
  • Star Wars Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords

Zach Sharpe

Zach Sharpe was born January 1990 and in turn, steeped in video games with the dawn of the Super Nintendo Entertainment System. His first games include Super Mario Brothers, Megaman X, and Clayfighters. Since, his love for video games has continually evolved taking major turns with Final Fantasy 7 & 9 towards Japanese role-playing games, and later World of Warcraft opening up a passion for MMORPGs. Most recently, League of Legends, Heroes of the Storm, and Overwatch have proven a competitive enjoyment of video games for Zach as well.

In addition to gaming, Zach spends most of his time with his wife and tending to his newborn son at their house in Thompson’s Station, TN. Software development, music, and reading rank among Zach’s favorite past times.

Top 3 Games

  • Final Fantasy 7
  • World of Warcraft
  • Breath of the Wild
